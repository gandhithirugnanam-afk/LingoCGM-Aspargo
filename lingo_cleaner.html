Lingo CGM Cleaner & Meal Detector

Parses an Abbott Lingo glucose export, removes unreliable start-up / warm-up data, and flags likely meal (food-intake) time points — all in your browser. No data leaves this page.

1. Load your Lingo CSV

Drop your lingoglucosedata….csv here, or click to choose a file.

2. Cleaning & detection settings

Warm-up: readings within the first N hours after each sensor starts are discarded — Abbott itself hides the first ~1 h and accuracy is poor early on. Range: values outside the sensor's reportable range are treated as errors. Meal: a sustained rise of ≥ threshold within the window marks a probable eating event.
